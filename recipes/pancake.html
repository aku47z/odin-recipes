Pancake Recipe

Description

Pancakes are simple and fun to make and a delight to eat. Good pancakes can easily be your favorite breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Vanilla essence
  • Baking powder

Steps

  1. Separate egg yolk from egg whites and stir the egg whites till they become foamy.
  2. Mix 4 tablespoons of sugar, a tablespoon of baking powder, 2 tablespoons of butter and a pinch of salt with the egg whites.
  3. Mix egg yolk with half a cup of flour and then mix with the foamy egg white mixture to make the pancake batter. Do not overmix. Finally add a little vanilla essence to the batter.
  4. Let the batter rest for at least fifteen minutes.
  5. Heat up a pan on low heat and grease it with butter.
  6. Gently pour the pancake batter onto the pan into circle shapes.
  7. Flip the pancake after a few minutes when bubbles stop forming on the surface of the pancake. Take out the pancake after a minute.
  8. Voila! Your pancakes are ready. Enjoy them with butter, fruits, jam, ice cream or just as they are.
