Egg Sandwich Recipe

Description

Egg Sandwich is an easy meal you can make within a few minutes. Simple yet delicious, eggs between breads can never go wrong together.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheese slices
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Beat eggs and mix a teaspoon of salt with the eggs.
  2. Heat up a tablespoon of cooking oil on a pan and pour the eggs. Scramble the eggs as they are cooking to make scrambled eggs.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on breads and add cheese slices on top of them.
  4. Put the scrambled eggs on top of the breads evenly and put another bread on top to make a sandwich.
  5. Grill the sandwich on a pan or heat them in a microwave or use a sandwich maker.
  6. Enjoy your egg sandwiches!
